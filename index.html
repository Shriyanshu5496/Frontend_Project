How It Works
What is an ATS?

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software used by employers to scan and rank resumes.

How to use this tool:
  1. Upload your resume in PDF format
  2. Select the job type you're applying for
  3. Click "Check ATS" to analyze your resume
What we check:
  • Keyword matching with job requirements
  • Resume formatting and structure
  • Education and experience sections
  • Action verbs and achievements
